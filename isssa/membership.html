Society Governance & Elections

ISSSA is a democratically governed academic society. We believe in transparent leadership elected by the very academics who contribute to our community.

Next Election Cycle:

The next triennial Executive Committee elections will be held at the General Body Meeting in December 2026.

The Executive Committee (EC)

The daily administration of the society and the INJASSS editorial board is managed by the EC, consisting of the President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer, and four Joint Members.

Electoral College & Voting

Only registered "Professional Life Members" are eligible to vote. Voting is conducted via secret ballot during the triennial General Body Meeting. Proxy voting is strictly prohibited.

Standing for Office

Any Life Member who has been in good standing with the society for a minimum of 3 consecutive years is eligible to submit a nomination to run for a position on the Executive Committee.

Membership Guidelines & Conduct

All members are expected to uphold the highest standards of academic integrity. Membership may be revoked by a two-thirds majority vote of the EC if a member is found guilty of plagiarism, research fabrication, or conduct detrimental to the society's reputation.
