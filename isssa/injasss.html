Author Guidelines

Before submitting your manuscript, please ensure it complies with the INJASSS academic standards to avoid desk rejection.

Manuscript Formatting

Submissions must be in English. Use standard APA or MLA formatting. Manuscripts should be between 2,500 and 6,000 words, including references and abstract. Documents must be in .doc or .docx format.

Double-Blind Review

INJASSS utilizes a strict double-blind peer review process governed by our Advisory Council. Ensure that the main manuscript file does not contain author names or affiliations.

Originality & Plagiarism

All submissions are screened via Turnitin. The maximum acceptable similarity index is 10%. Articles found to have manipulated data or plagiarized content will be permanently banned.
