INJASSS

Indian National Journal of Advanced Social Science Studies

The official flagship publication of the International Social Sciences Studies Association (ISSSA), providing a premier platform for rigorous, peer-reviewed academic discourse.

Open Access Double-Blind Peer Review Quarterly Publication ISSN: Applied For

Aims and Scope

INJASSS bridges the analytical gaps between diverse social science disciplines. We are dedicated to publishing high-quality empirical research, theoretical reviews, and methodological advancements that shape our understanding of human society, spatial planning, and developmental economics.

Primary Domains Covered

  • Geography & Spatial Studies: Human resource development, GIS mapping, urban planning, and demographic shifts.
  • Political Science & Governance: Public policy analysis, international relations, parliamentary studies, and institutional frameworks.
  • Economics & Sociology: Developmental economics, social stratification, cultural studies, and resource allocation.
  • Interdisciplinary Studies: Research that cross-pollinates methodologies from multiple social science fields.

Types of Submissions Accepted

  • Original Research Articles: Full-length empirical studies introducing new data or theoretical frameworks (3,000 - 6,000 words).
  • Review Papers: Comprehensive analyses of existing literature on highly relevant social science topics.
  • Short Communications & Case Studies: Brief reports on specific regional phenomena, policy implementations, or preliminary data findings (1,500 - 3,000 words).

Editorial Policies & Guidelines

Double-Blind Peer Review

To ensure objectivity, INJASSS strictly adheres to a double-blind review process. The identities of both the authors and the reviewers are concealed. Manuscripts are evaluated by at least two independent experts from our Advisory Council based on originality, methodology, and relevance.

Formatting Standards

Submissions must be written in clear, concise English. Authors must follow either APA (7th Edition) or MLA formatting for in-text citations and references. Manuscripts should be submitted as Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) files, typed in Times New Roman, 12pt, with 1.5 line spacing.

Plagiarism & Originality

INJASSS maintains a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism. All submitted manuscripts undergo rigorous similarity checks using industry-standard software (e.g., Turnitin). The maximum acceptable similarity index is 10%. Articles found to contain plagiarized material will be immediately rejected.

Open Access Policy

We believe in the free dissemination of academic knowledge. INJASSS provides immediate open access to its content on the principle that making research freely available to the public supports a greater global exchange of knowledge.

Copyright & Licensing

Authors retain the copyright of their published work. Articles are distributed under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution License (CC BY 4.0), which permits unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited.

Publication Timeline

We strive for efficiency without compromising rigorous review. Authors can expect an initial desk decision within 7-10 days. The complete peer-review process typically takes 4-6 weeks from submission to final acceptance notification.
