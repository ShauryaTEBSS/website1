Academic Excellence & Research

Our Mission

ISSSA bridges the gap between diverse fields of social science, from geography and political science to economics and sociology. We provide a rigorous platform for established scholars and emerging researchers to debate, discover, and disseminate knowledge that shapes our understanding of human society.

Governed by an esteemed advisory council of leading professors, our association upholds the highest standards of peer-reviewed academic integrity.

Flagship Publication

INJASSS (Indian National Journal of Advanced Social Science Studies) is the official peer-reviewed journal of the ISSSA.

Publishing quarterly, INJASSS accepts high-quality empirical and theoretical papers across all branches of the social sciences. We are currently accepting submissions for our upcoming volume.

Policies & Governance

Society Registration

The International Social Sciences Studies Association (ISSSA) operates in accordance with the regulations set forth for educational and research societies. It is headquartered in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Open Access Policy

ISSSA is committed to the free dissemination of knowledge. All research articles published in INJASSS are available online to readers immediately upon publication without subscription barriers.

Peer Review Ethics

We adhere strictly to a double-blind peer review process. All submitted manuscripts are evaluated by independent academic experts on our advisory board to ensure validity, significance, and originality.

Plagiarism & Originality

ISSSA utilizes industry-standard similarity checking software. Manuscripts found to contain unoriginal content without proper citation will be immediately rejected and the authors barred from future submissions.
