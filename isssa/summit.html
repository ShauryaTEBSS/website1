About The Movement

The Earth is undergoing unprecedented ecological stress. Under the aegis of the ISSSA, the National Save The Earth Movement Summit acts as an apex gathering for geographers, environmental scientists, urban planners, and coders to forge holistic solutions.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced technology and human resource development. By uniting mapping technologies, social sciences, and grassroots activism, we aim to map, monitor, and mitigate ecological degradation across the Indian subcontinent.

Core Domains

Summit Research Tracks

Researchers are invited to submit abstracts and full papers to the INJASSS editorial board under the following four critical environmental domains.

GIS & Remote Sensing

Applying satellite imagery and QGIS/ArcGIS for monitoring deforestation, watershed management, and climate modeling.

Green IoT Tech

Deploying Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers for real-time air quality, soil moisture, and ecological data collection.

Human Geography

Understanding the impact of human resource development on the environment and holistic societal progress.

Urban Sustainability

Reimagining city infrastructure, tackling pollution, and drafting policy frameworks for sustainable urban futures.
